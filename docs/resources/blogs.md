# Blogs & Articles

Curated posts worth reading on agentic systems.

## Foundational

- **[Building LLM applications for production](https://huyenchip.com/2023/04/11/llm-engineering.html)** — Chip Huyen
  Essential overview of LLM engineering challenges. Not agent-specific but foundational.

- **[What We Learned from a Year of Building with LLMs](https://www.oreilly.com/radar/what-we-learned-from-a-year-of-building-with-llms-part-i/)** — O'Reilly
  Practical lessons from production LLM systems.

## Security

- **[Not what you've signed up for: Compromising Real-World LLM-Integrated Applications with Indirect Prompt Injection](https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.12173)** — Greshake et al.
  The paper that named indirect prompt injection. Essential reading.

- **[Prompt Injection: What's the worst that can happen?](https://simonwillison.net/2023/Apr/14/worst-that-can-happen/)** — Simon Willison
  Clear explanation of prompt injection risks with concrete examples.

## Agent Architecture

- **[The Shift from Models to Compound AI Systems](https://bair.berkeley.edu/blog/2024/02/18/compound-ai-systems/)** — Berkeley AI Research
  Why single models aren't enough; case for compound systems.

- **[Cognitive Architectures for Language Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.02427)** — Sumers et al.
  Taxonomy of agent architectures. Good for understanding design space.

## Human-AI Interaction

- **[Why Chatbots Are Not the Future](https://wattenberger.com/thoughts/boo-chatbots)** — Amelia Wattenberger
  Thoughtful critique of chat interfaces; implications for agent UX.

## Newsletters

- **[The Batch](https://www.deeplearning.ai/the-batch/)** — DeepLearning.AI
  Weekly AI news with Andrew Ng's perspective.

- **[Last Week in AI](https://lastweekin.ai/)**
  Comprehensive weekly roundup.
